Bolt Wizard

Bolt Wizard streamlines rock support documentation for on-site workers. Capture a photo, mark installed bolts with your phone, and generate a detailed report effortlessly. The app supports various bolt diameters, lengths, and types based on Prosesskode 1 standards—while remaining adaptable for other uses. Once the report is completed, Bolt Wizard exports an annotated image and a summary table detailing all installed bolts. Designed for Android, iPhone, and desktop, it ensures precise, efficient, and hassle-free reporting for rock support operations. design, and is easy to use.
